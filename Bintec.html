Welcome to BINTEC

Bin Saud Technical Trading Est., (BINTEC) is registered in Abu Dhabi as Trading and Technical Services Company. We are located in Tourist Club Area, Abu Dhabi, U.A.E with Workshop/Testing facility located in Mussafah Industrial Area.

Bintec is an ISO 9001:2000 certified company and managed by a team of Technically Qualified Professionals with over two decades of experience in Fire/Gas Detection, Safety Equipments and Fire Fighting System for Oil / Gas, Marine and Industrial Sectors of the United Arab Emirates.

BINTEC is in Business for over 30 years, during this period we have gained the trust and goodwill of our various Clients, our Client base continues to grow not just in U.A.E but in neighboring Countries as well. Our range of Products and Services has also grown to meet different market demands. However our core business continues to be in the fields of Fire and Gas Detection, Safety Equipment and Fire Fighting Systems.

Our Client base in UAE includes practically all Oil and Gas Production/Refining Companies, many Ship owners/Operators as well as other major Companies. Read More...
